Puppy School is a network of reward-based trainers led by internationally renowned dog behaviourist and trainer, Gwen Bailey.

Puppy School Crawley run fun, family-friendly classes for puppies up to 20 weeks of age, using reward based scientifically proven and effective methods:

Recall
Learn to come back when called
Basic commands
Sit, stay, lay down, and stand positions
Socialisation
& Play
Being around other people and dogs
Loose lead walking
Walk calmly without pulling on the lead
Handling
Getting used to being touched and held
Enrichment
Games designed to challenge your puppy
And much more...
