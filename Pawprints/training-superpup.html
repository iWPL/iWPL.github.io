Puppy School is a network of reward-based trainers led by internationally renowned dog behaviourist and trainer, Gwen Bailey.

Run by Puppy School Crawley, Super Pup is a 6-week follow-on course for puppier and young dogs who have completed the Puppy School course.

Super Speed
Quicker recall and more focus on walks
Super Senses
Simple nose work games and building confidence around scary noises
Super Strength and Fitness
Body maintenance and doggy workouts
Super Stealth and Stillness
Teaching Calmness
Super Flexibility
On things, over things, under things, having fun!
Super Social
Building an unbeatable team and socialisation challenges throughout the 6 weeks
