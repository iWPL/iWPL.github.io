Pet Photography

1 hour

Pawprints Dog Training & Pet Care Services has teamsed up with the amazing Darrell Hobbs Photography to capture that perfect pet portrait.

Allowing your pet to feel relaxed and comfortable is an essential first step in being able to capture the best photographs. Using calm and empathetic positive reinforcement to help your pet feel at ease, Darrell always gets good results!

Bespoke Sessions

Bespoke photography sessions for you and your pet can be arranged in a suitable indoor or outdoor location in Horley, Crawley or surrounding areas.

Action shots, scenic photos, and studio style portraits can all be done, as well as full family pictures. Moments you will treasure forever.

Why not combine a photography session with a Pawprints one-to-one training session, or a Pawprints dog walk?
