Pet Care and Home Visits

Company and care for your pet

Walkies!

1-4 dogs
Local to Horley & Crawley
1 hour

Pawprints Dog Training & Pet Care Services will take your dog(s) for a walk during the weekday. (Ad hoc weekend and bank holiday walks possible by special request).

Solo or small compatible group (up to 4) dog walks are available.

Pawprints will meet you and your dog(s) for an initial FREE consultation to learn their needs and establish compatibility.

Safe

Walks are in safe locations

  • Away from roads
  • Woodland
  • Grasslands
  • Parks

Solo walks from home on pavements can be requested.

Fun

Enriching and mentally stimulating walks offer a balance of

  • Running
  • Sniffing
  • Playing
  • Relaxed mooching

Routine

Your dog(s) will be safely transported between your home and the days walk location.

After their Pawprints adventure they will return satisfied, fullfilled and ready to relax but not before they’ve been given a quick clean.

Duration

Walks will typically be at least 1 hour. Shorter trots or longer adventures are also available.

For the health and safety of your dog(s) extremes of weather (heat, rain, stroms, cold, etc.) may cut the walk short. In these cases a Pet Sitting home visit can be arranged instead.

The not-so-small print

All Pawprints dogs must be up to date with their vaccinations (including Kennel cough) and their routine flea and worming treatments.

Proof will be required during the initial FREE consultation

New dogs will be walked on a long training lead whilst a trusting relationship is built. They will only be allowed off-lead once recall is consistent
